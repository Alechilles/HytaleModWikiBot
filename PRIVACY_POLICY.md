# HytaleModWikiBot Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data HytaleModWikiBot (the "Bot") processes and why.

## 1. Data We Process

The Bot processes the following data to operate:
- Discord server (guild) IDs
- Discord user IDs related to command use
- Command inputs (for example, wiki queries)
- Bot-generated lookup outputs (for example, resolved mod/page slug and URL)
- Guild configuration data (aliases, default mods, visibility/embed preferences)
- Operational metadata (for example, timestamps, latency, outcome status, rate-limit events, errors)

The Bot does not require or intentionally collect message content outside command interactions.

## 2. How Data Is Used

Data is used to:
- Execute commands and return wiki results
- Store guild-specific configuration (aliases/defaults/preferences)
- Enforce rate limits and prevent abuse
- Diagnose errors, monitor performance, and improve matching quality

## 3. Data Storage and Retention

Bot data is stored in the operator's configured database (PostgreSQL).
Retention is based on operational needs and may change over time.
Data may be deleted, rotated, or reset during maintenance, migration, abuse handling, or service shutdown.

## 4. Data Sharing

The Bot does not sell personal data.
Data may be shared only as necessary to provide the service through third-party infrastructure (such as Discord and hosting/database providers), or when required by law.

## 5. Third-Party Services

The Bot interacts with third-party services:
- Discord (platform and API)
- `wiki.hytalemodding.dev` (wiki content source and optional API search)

Those services have their own privacy policies and practices.

## 6. Security

Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are used, but no method of storage or transmission is guaranteed to be fully secure.

## 7. Your Choices

Server admins can remove the Bot at any time.
If removed, previously stored data may persist for a period based on operational retention practices.

For data/privacy requests, open an issue:
- [https://github.com/Alechilles/HytaleModWikiBot/issues](https://github.com/Alechilles/HytaleModWikiBot/issues)

## 8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
Continued use of the Bot after updates become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
